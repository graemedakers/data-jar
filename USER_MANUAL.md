## 1. Introduction

Date Jar allows you and your partner to create a shared collection of date ideas. When you're ready for a date, simply "spin the jar" to get a random suggestion based on your preferences (time, cost, location).

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Sign Up
1.  Navigate to the landing page.
2.  Click the **"Get Started"** button.
3.  Enter your names (e.g., "Alex & Sam").
4.  Enter your email address and a secure password.
5.  **Location:** Enter your city (e.g., "New York, NY"). This helps us suggest relevant ideas.
6.  Click **"Create Jar"**.

**Note:** You only need one account per couple. You can share the login, or invite your partner to create their own linked account (see Section 7).

---

## 3. The Dashboard

Once logged in, you will see your **Dashboard**.
- **The Jar:** A 3D representation of your jar. It fills up as you add ideas!
- **Stats:** Shows how many ideas are currently in the jar.
- **Action Buttons:** "Add Idea" and "Spin Jar".
- **Lists:**
    - **In the Jar:** Active ideas waiting to be picked.
    - **Past Dates:** Ideas you have already completed.

---

## 4. Adding Date Ideas

You can add ideas manually or let our AI suggest them.

### Manual Entry
1.  Click the **"Add Idea"** button (Plus icon).
2.  **Description:** Type a short title (e.g., "Picnic in the Park").
3.  **Setting:** Choose "Indoor" or "Outdoor".
4.  **Duration:** Select how long the date will take (e.g., "2 hours").
5.  **Cost:** Select the price range (Free, $, $$, $$$).
6.  **Energy:** Select the activity level (Chill, Moderate, Active).
7.  **Time:** Select "Day", "Evening", or "Anytime".
8.  Click **"Add to Jar"**.

### AI "Surprise Me"
1.  Click **"Add Idea"**.
2.  Click the **"Surprise Me"** button (Sparkles icon).
3.  Wait a moment for the AI to generate a creative date idea.
4.  Review the details and click **"Add to Jar"**.

---

## 5. Spinning the Jar

Ready for a date?
1.  Click the **"Spin Jar"** button.
2.  **Filters (Optional):**
    - You can filter by **Duration** (e.g., "Under 2 hours").
    - You can filter by **Cost** (e.g., "Free only").
    - You can filter by **Setting** (Indoor/Outdoor).
3.  Click **"Spin!"**.
4.  Watch the animation...
5.  **The Reveal:** Your date idea will appear!
6.  Click **"Accept Date"** to mark it as selected. This moves it from the Jar to your History.

---

## 6. Managing History

### Past Dates
Scroll down on the dashboard to see **Past Dates**.
- **Rate:** Click the stars to rate how much you enjoyed the date.
- **Repeat:** Click the **Copy** icon to add the idea back into the jar for another time.
- **Delete:** Click the **Trash** icon to remove the entry from your history permanently.

### Deletion Log
To see a record of deleted items:
1.  Click the **Settings** icon (Gear) in the top right.
2.  Click **"View Deletion History"**.
3.  You will see a list of who deleted what and when.

---

## 7. Settings & Inviting Your Partner

### Invite Partner
1.  On the Dashboard, look for the **"Invite Code"** at the top right.
2.  Click the code to copy the invite link.
3.  Send this link to your partner. When they sign up, they will automatically be joined to your jar!

### Update Location
1.  Click the **Settings** icon.
2.  Update the **"Your Location"** field.
3.  Click **"Save Changes"**.

### Danger Zone
- **Empty Jar:** In Settings, you can click "Empty Jar" to delete ALL ideas and history. **Warning: This cannot be undone.**
