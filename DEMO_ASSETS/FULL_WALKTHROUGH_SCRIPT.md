## Scene 2: The Dashboard & Adding Ideas
**Visual:**
- **Screen:** Dashboard.
- **Action:** Hover over the 3D Jar.
- **Action:** Click **"Add Idea"**.
- **Screen:** Add Idea Modal.
- **Action:** Type "Homemade Pizza Night", select "Indoor", "Medium Energy". Click "Add to Jar".
- **Action:** Click **"Add Idea"** again.
- **Action:** Click **"Surprise Me"** (Premium Feature). Wait for AI generation. Click "Add to Jar".

**Voiceover:**
"The dashboard is your central hub. You can add your own ideas manually, or if you're feeling stuck, use our Premium AI 'Surprise Me' button to instantly generate creative date concepts."

## Scene 3: The Spin
**Visual:**
- **Action:** Click **"Spin Jar"**.
- **Screen:** Filter Modal.
- **Action:** Toggle "Indoor Only" on and off. Click **"Spin!"**.
- **Screen:** Spinning Animation -> Result Card.
- **Action:** Read the result. Click **"Accept Date"**.

**Voiceover:**
"Ready for a date? Hit 'Spin Jar'. You can apply filters if you have specific preferences. The jar selects a random idea, adding an element of surprise to your evening."

## Scene 4: Premium Planning Tools
**Visual:**

## Scene 5: History & Settings
**Visual:**

- **Screen:** History Section.

- **Action:** Show the "Invite Partner" code. Close Settings.

**Voiceover:**
"Keep track of your memories in the History section, where you can rate your dates. In Settings, you can find your unique code to invite your partner, so you can share the same jar."
