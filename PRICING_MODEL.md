# Recommended Pricing Model: "Date Jar Pro"

## Core Philosophy
**Simplicity & Sustainability.** 
Users are hesitant to subscribe to "yet another app," so the value proposition must be clear. A low-cost monthly subscription covers your AI API costs, while a higher Lifetime tier captures revenue from those who refuse subscriptions (often 20-30% of user base).

## The Model Structure

### 1. The "Free Forever" Base Tier
This ensures you have a wide funnel of users.
*   **Jar Limit:** 1 Active Jar (Romantic OR Group).
*   **Member Limit:** Max 4 Members (Good for couples or small squads).
*   **Features:** Basic "Spin the Jar", Pre-filled Ideas, Syncing.
*   **Restriction:** No AI features (Dining Concierge, Weekend Planner) after trial.

### 2. The "14-Day Free Trial" (The Hook)
*   **Trigger:** Starts automatically upon sign-up or first "Pro" feature attempt.
*   **Experience:** Full access to **Date Jar Pro** features.
*   **No Credit Card Required** (Low friction) or **Card Required** (Higher conversion quality). *Recommendation: No card required for maximizing growth.*

### 3. "Date Jar Pro" Subscription (Recurring Revenue)
*   **Price:** **AU$3.99 / month** (Price of a coffee - psychological "no brainer").
*   **Features:**
    *   **Unlimited Jars:** Have a romantic jar, a family jar, and multiple friend jars.
    *   **Unlimited Members:** Great for large friend groups (hens/bucks parties, travel groups).
    *   **AI Intelligence:** Full access to Smart Weekend Planner, Dining Concierge, and Bar Scout.
    *   **Advanced Stats:** See who spins the most, favorite categories, etc.

### 4. "Lifetime Unlock" (Cash Injection)
*   **Price:** **AU$59.99** (Equivalent to ~15 months of subscription).
*   **Philosophy:** Captures the "I hate subscriptions" crowd.
*   **Profitability:** Most users churn from apps within 6-12 months. Getting 15 months of revenue upfront is usually a win for the developer, even with ongoing AI costs (as usage typically drops off over time).
