# Date Jar - Introduction Video Script

**Video Title:** Date Jar: Spice Up Your Relationship
**Duration:** ~3 Minutes

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)

**Visual:**
- Show the **Landing Page** (`/`).
- Camera pans over the "Date Jar" logo and the tagline "Spice Up Your Relationship".
- Focus on the 3D Jar animation spinning slowly.

**Voiceover:**
"Are you and your partner tired of asking, 'What do you want to do tonight?' only to hear 'I don't know' back? Meet **Date Jar**, the fun, interactive way to decide on your next date."

---

## Scene 2: Getting Started (0:30 - 1:00)

**Visual:**
- Click **"Get Started"** button.
- Show the **Sign Up** form.
- Type in a demo name (e.g., "Alex"), email, and password.
- Highlight the **"Your Location"** field (e.g., typing "New York").
- Click **"Create Jar"**.

**Voiceover:**
"Getting started is easy. Just create an account. You'll get your very own digital jar. We also ask for your location to help you find great spots nearby."

---

## Scene 3: Adding Ideas (1:00 - 2:00)

**Visual:**
- Show the **Dashboard** (empty jar state).
- Click the big **"Add Idea"** button.
- **Manual Entry:** Type "Picnic in the Park". Select "Outdoor", "2 hours", "Free", "Day". Click "Add to Jar".
- **AI Entry:** Click "Add Idea" again. Click the **"Surprise Me"** button.
- Show the loading spinner and then a generated idea (e.g., "Pottery Class"). Click "Add to Jar".

**Voiceover:**
"Now, fill your jar! You can add your own favorite date ideas, setting details like cost, duration, and whether it's indoor or outdoor.
Running low on inspiration? Hit the **'Surprise Me'** button to let our AI suggest a creative date idea for you."

---

## Scene 4: The Spin (2:00 - 2:45)

**Visual:**
- Click the **"Spin Jar"** button.
- Show the **Filter Modal**. Briefly show that you can filter by "Indoor only" or "Under $".
- Click **"Spin!"**.
- Show the jar spinning animation.
- Show the **Reveal Card** popping up with confetti.
- Click **"Accept Date"**.

**Voiceover:**
"When it's time for your next date, just spin the jar! You can even add filters if you're on a budget or want to stay indoors. The jar picks a random idea from your collection. No more indecision!"

---

## Scene 5: History & Management (2:45 - 3:30)

**Visual:**
- Show the dashboard with the idea now moved to **"Past Dates"**.
- Click the **Settings** (gear icon).
- Show the **"View Deletion History"** button.
- Open the log to show a record of changes.
- Show the **Invite Code** at the top.

**Voiceover:**
"Keep track of your memories in the history tab. You can rate your dates and even invite your partner to join your jar using a simple invite code, so you can both add ideas and spin together."

---

## Scene 6: Outro (3:30 - 3:45)

**Visual:**
- Return to the **Landing Page**.
- Show the URL: `date-jar.vercel.app`.

**Voiceover:**
"Stop planning and start dating. Try Date Jar today!"

---
